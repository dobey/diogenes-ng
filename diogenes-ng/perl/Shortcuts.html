In addition to the browser shortcuts shown in the menus, there are several more keyboard shortcuts that you can use on the main search page. Use with the control or apple key.

  • L Choose the PHI Latin Corpus
  • G Choose the TLG
  • D Choose the Duke Papyri
  • O Search with the TLG word list
  • E Choose the Coptic corpus
  • . Choose a simple search
  • U Choose a multiple search
  • B Choose to browse
  • R Choose to manage corpora
